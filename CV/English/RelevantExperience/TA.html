Position: Teacher Assistant (personal page)
Employer: Uppsala University

Description of the job:

Before the summer of 2023 I enrolled in the course Programming Technique II 5Hp and was able to get the highest grade in the course. Afterwards, the teacher recommended me to be a teacher assistant for the course given in the autumn of 2023. After considering the pros and cons of the job I made an application for the position of TA.

Since then and until now I have been working as a TA for programming courses. The courses are both at bachelor's level and master's level My job is to help the students with their assignments and answer any questions they have. I also help the teacher with correcting the assignments and giving feedback to the students. There has even been times when I have been tasked with grading the students' examination.
